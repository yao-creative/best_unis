The rankings on the University Review site are derived by weighting publicly available data supplied by the USnews, Times, and Jiao Tong. It is hoped that the methodology used helps to eliminate some of the strange results that occur due to these sources using different weightings in their algorhythms, for example the Jiao Tong is more biased to research and graduate level study whereas the USnews has a bias towards undergraduate study.

Of course any top 100 colleges list is bound to be subjective, it is therefore recommended that you use these listings only as a starting point in your search for a college, and that you find out as much about any higher education establishment that you plan to attend as possible, particularly with how it ranks in the subject area that you intend to pursue. Ideally, you should visit any college and take a look around and speak to students attending the place before making your final choice.
